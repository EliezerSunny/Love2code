Love2Code is your FREE site for learning how to code. Our goal is to help you find your way, quickly and easily, in the confusing world of learning to code. There are so many resources online with varying levels of difficulty that it is often hard to find something for those just getting started.

Take a look and find a section of the site that is a fit for you. Learning to code is time consuming, so we want you to focus your time on the resources that will get you your next raise, help with a career change or build out your million dollar idea as quickly as possible.

What is Coding? For those of you just getting started, this is the perfect place for you.
