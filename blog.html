Introducing Love2Code Blog

Updated: Mar 18, 2022

Advertisement

We’ve been hard at work here at Love2Code and are excited to announce a few changes.

What will we cover on our blog? Let’s take a look.

A Focus on Desktop and Mobile Web Development

With all the programming languages we could help our readers become more proficient at, we feel the most beneficial are web based programming languages focused on building desktop websites, mobile optimized websites and apps.

So, like all programmers, we want to reduce redundancy and give you the best content for the little amount of time you have.

Announcing New and Updated Content

Previously, if we released new content, you may not have known about it. Now, we have a forum to announce all content updates and newly released content. For example, stay tuned for revamped learn to code reviews of all of the current players in the industry like TeamTreeHouse.com, w3schools.com, Freecodecamp.org, CodeSchool.com and Codecademy.com.

Industry News

Having a hard time keeping up with the latest in programming news, tools, tips and helpful resources? Don’t fret, let us be your one stop shop for industry news and content. We’ll create the best information that will help you the most on your journey to mastering a language or becoming a coding ninja.

Development Tips

Everyone likes to hear what others are doing to become more efficient as a coder. One of our favorite pieces of content to create and share are development tips to help improve on all aspects of coding.
